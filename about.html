About Me

I am an aspiring Data Analyst with a passion for lifelong learning. My expertise lies in Excel, MySQL and Power BI which I used to turn raw data into meaningful insights. I always keep exploring new career fields and opportunities, and looking for ways to challenge myself and reach new heights.
When I'm not diving into the latest finance and business books, you can find me expanding my knowledge on investing, and looking for opportunities to connect with industry experts in the future.

Following my unsuccessful attempt at the Engineering Entrance Exams after class 12, I was faced with a difficult decision. I could either take a gap year to prepare for the exams or take admission in a private college. However, due to financial constraints, the latter was not an option. Despite this, I chose to take a gap year and dedicated eight months to self-reflection and personal growth. In April 2022, I enrolled in the Online BS Degree Programme at IIT Madras, which was a significant turning point in my academic and professional journey.

Since then, I have become a goal-oriented individual with a passion for exploring various domains in tech, including Web Development, Backend Development, Data Science, and Data Analytics. My degree in this field has given me a solid foundation in Data-driven skills (DS and DA), leading me to choose Data Analytics as my primary focus.

Today, I am more focused on building a portfolio of projects that showcase my expertise and learning advanced concepts to enhance my skills further. While I may not have achieved any significant accomplishments to date, I strive to do great things every day, slowly and steadily.
